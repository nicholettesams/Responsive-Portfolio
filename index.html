About Me

For over 14 years, I have worked on the Infinite Visions line of products for Tyler Technologies. Infinite Visions is an ERP suite for K-12 school districts used by over 1300 clients across the United States. I'm currently the Development Manger for the Finance modules.

I received my Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science from Bowling Green State University in Ohio in 2003. Go Falcons!

Euclid, Ohio is my home town, however, I spent the last 15 years living in Phoenix, Arizona. I just moved back to Kirtland, Ohio in June.

In my spare time I am a wife and mother. I also belong to my alumni sorority group for Phi Mu Fraternity. I enjoy volunteering my time for philanthropic causes through Phi Mu.

I am a gamer and I enjoy going to movies. I love food, but I hate to cook!
